Go Figure

This project involved designing and exploring the concept of GoFigure, a smart urban system combining illuminated pathways, interactive screens, and a mobile app to enhance safety and social presence on the way to Nydalasjön, a lake in Umeå, helping visitors feel more secure, enjoy their journey, and experience the Northern Lights in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Illuminate Ålidhem

This project involved collaborating with Umeå Kommun and local stakeholders to explore how improved lighting, wall art, and activity centers could enhance the user experience in a public urban space, especially during the long, grey winters. By using user research and interviews, we aimed to create a more inviting, engaging, and lively environment that meets community needs and encourages social interaction.
